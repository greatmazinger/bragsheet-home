College Counseling Tool

BragSheet

BragSheet gives students a structured way to reflect on their achievements, experiences, and personal qualities, and share that raw material directly with their school counselor. There's no processing or interpretation: what the student writes is what the counselor receives, in the student's own words, ready to inform a recommendation letter that genuinely reflects who they are.

Coming soon on Android and iOS.

About

We build what students and counselors actually need.

Athena Lexi Studios was founded by a husband-and-wife team in the San Francisco Bay Area who watched the college application process up close, and saw how much support was out of reach for families without access to expensive private counselors.

Our tools are designed to lower that barrier. We use AI to do the structural and organizational work, so the human voices - students, counselors, families - stay at the center of every outcome.

We are a small independent studio. Every tool we ship is something we've used ourselves or built in direct response to a real need we encountered.

Student-centered design

Our tools make space for each student's individual story, not a template version of it.

Accessibility first

We build for families and schools at all income levels, not only those with private college counselors.

AI that assists, not replaces

We use AI for structure and prompting - the student's and counselor's own words always come first.

Privacy and trust

Student data stays private. We build to the standards families and schools deserve.
