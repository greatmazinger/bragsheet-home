Data Deletion

How to delete your BragSheet data  ·  Applies to: BragSheet (Android, iOS)

BragSheet stores all your data locally on your device — there are no user accounts and no servers holding your information. You can delete everything at any time using the steps below.

How to delete your data on Android

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Tap Apps (or Application Manager)
  3. Find and tap BragSheet
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data — this permanently deletes all brag sheets, answers, audio recordings, and settings stored by the app

Alternatively, uninstalling BragSheet removes all locally stored data from your device.

What data is deleted and what is retained

Data type Outcome Details
Brag sheets & answers Deleted Permanently removed from your device immediately
Audio recordings Deleted Permanently removed from your device immediately
App settings Deleted Permanently removed from your device immediately
Anonymous analytics events Retained (anonymous) If you opted into telemetry, anonymous usage events were sent to PostHog. These events contain no personal information and cannot be linked to you. They are retained for up to 12 months per PostHog's data retention policy.
