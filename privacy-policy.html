Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2025  ·  Applies to: BragSheet (Android, iOS)

Athena Lexi Studios ("we," "our," or "us"), a division of Athena Lexi Ventures, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the BragSheet app handles information. Please read it carefully.

1. Information we collect

BragSheet does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All information entered into the app — including student responses, achievements, and profile details — is stored locally on the user's device only.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Student academic records or identifying information
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Any data through account registration (BragSheet has no accounts)

2. How information is used

Because all data stays on your device, we do not use, process, sell, or share any personal information. We have no access to what you enter into BragSheet.

3. Sharing with counselors

BragSheet allows students to share their completed profile with a school counselor. This sharing is initiated entirely by the student and takes place outside the app — for example, by exporting and sending the information via email or another method the student chooses. Athena Lexi Studios is not involved in or party to that transfer in any way.

4. Children's privacy

BragSheet is intended for use by high school students in connection with the college application process. Because the app does not collect any personal data from any users — including minors — it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We never knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Third-party services

BragSheet does not integrate with or send data to any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services.

6. Data security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, your information is protected by your device's own security features. We encourage users to use a screen lock and keep their device software up to date.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of BragSheet after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
